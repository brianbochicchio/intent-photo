Getting started

Tap the "+" icon at the top of most screens to start a new entry. Intent will prompt you to take or attach a photo. Add notes and optionally set a location. When you save, coordinates and weather are recorded automatically.

Only for getting the weather. Otherwise Intent works entirely offline. All data is stored locally on your device.

Yes. Location is optional. You can create entries without it, though location data is one of the core features of the field archive. You can adjust permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Data & backups

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your Intent data is included in your standard iOS device backup automatically. We recommend keeping iCloud Backup enabled to protect your field archive.

Yes. Intent exports a ZIP archive that you can AirDrop or email to yourself. The archive is organized in a human‑ and machine‑friendly format using Markdown.

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your data will be restored when you reinstall and restore from backup. Always ensure backups are current before removing the app.

No. Intent does not collect personal data. All information remains stored locally on your device. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Audio & photos

Audio files are stored locally in the app’s Documents directory on your device. They are included in iCloud Backup automatically if it is enabled.

Reference photos are saved as optimized JPEGs (max 2000px, 75% quality) to keep your archive manageable. They're intended as context anchors, not finished images.
